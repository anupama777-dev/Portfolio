eFlow Doc

Overview

The "e-Flow Doc" is an innovative solution designed to simplify and enhance the process of document submission, storage, verification, and marking. Leveraging modern technologies and a user-centric approach, this system aims to streamline and automate various document-related tasks, reducing manual effort and increasing efficiency.
At its core, the "e-Flow Doc" system automates the collection of document details from users through an intuitive electronic submission process. Users provide essential information and soft copies of their documents, which are securely stored and verified to ensure authenticity and integrity. The system also facilitates faculties to review and verify the uploaded documents.
